Bio:
TheraAI, is a project that was built in the large case, due to the increasing need of free-healthcare, and a more personalized, efficient system, that can connect with the exact health issues the patient is experiencing. Thus, TheraAI, takes these social-issues to hand, having no subscription, being accessible globally [via the internet], and having the capability of chatting with a patient or doctor on the basis of any condition.
